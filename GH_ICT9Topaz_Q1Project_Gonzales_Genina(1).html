CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Don Rafael Ibarra Don Rafael was Crisostomo Ibarra's father, but he passed away. He was a highly respected and the wealthiest person in San Diego. His character stood for the idea of human rights because he challenged the church's power and influence.
Don Santiago de los Santos Don Santiago de los Santos, who is also known as Kapitan Tiago, is a prosperous and influential Filipino figure. He plays the role of being Maria Clara's adoptive father and cares for her deeply.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is a Spanish friar who is corrupted and abuses his power for personal gain. He is a powerful and influential figure but cunning and manipulative.
Padre Salvi Padre Salvi is a Spanish friar who holds a good reputation among the people in San Diego. They see him as a responsible priest who takes his religious duties seriously. He is also looked up to as a leader in matters of faith and spirituality within the community.
Philosopher Tasio Philosopher Tasio is also known as Don Anastasyo, he is a wise and eccentric philosopher who provides guidance and critical thinking to the people.
